# Andrii QA Portfolio

This repository contains manual QA and product-support case-study materials: test plans, test cases, bug reports, usability reviews, support-triage examples, troubleshooting notes, and release-readiness templates. The goal is to show clear documentation, practical investigation, and the ability to turn confusing product problems into reproducible reports.

## Status

Portfolio v2 in progress. The e-commerce QA audit now includes real observed evidence from a limited manual review, while the remaining case studies are prepared as templates and sample workflows for future evidence.

## How to Read This Portfolio

This portfolio demonstrates manual QA, bug reporting, usability review, support triage, release-readiness judgment, and light technical troubleshooting. Case studies are organized so a reviewer can see scope, approach, evidence expectations, findings format, and recommended next steps.

## Evidence Policy

Real case studies should include:

- Tested URL or app name
- Date tested
- Device, browser, and OS
- Test scope and out-of-scope areas
- Screenshots or Loom recording when available
- Test cases
- Findings or bug reports
- Summary and recommendations
- Privacy/redaction notes where screenshots are used

Do not treat a case study as real evidence until the tested URL/app name, date, environment, actual results, and supporting evidence are added.
