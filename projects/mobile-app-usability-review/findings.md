# Mobile App Usability Findings

## Top Issues

1. The onboarding flow does not clearly explain the final action.
2. Some validation messages are easy to miss.
3. Password requirements should be visible before submission.
4. Back navigation should preserve entered information when possible.
5. Help or support options should be easier to find during setup.

## Support Notes

These issues could create avoidable support questions from users who are unsure whether their account was created, why their password failed, or what to do after onboarding.
