## Scope

Sample usability review for a mobile onboarding and account setup flow.

## Findings

| ID | Finding | Severity | Impact | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| UX-001 | Primary action label is unclear on the final onboarding screen | Medium | New users may not know what happens next | Use a direct label such as "Create account" or "Start setup" |
| UX-002 | Error messages disappear too quickly | Medium | Users may miss what needs to be corrected | Keep validation messages visible until the field is fixed |
| UX-003 | Password requirements are only shown after an error | Low | Users must fail before learning the rule | Show password requirements before submission |
| UX-004 | Back navigation behavior is unclear between steps | Medium | Users may worry that going back will erase entered information | Add clear back navigation and preserve entered data when possible |
| UX-005 | Help or support path is hard to find during setup | Low | Users with questions may abandon the flow instead of asking for help | Add a visible help link or short support prompt near confusing steps |
