# Support Ticket: Unexpected Billing Charge

## Customer Issue

User reports an unexpected billing charge and wants to understand why it happened.

## Clarifying Questions

- What date and amount does the customer see?
- Which plan or subscription is active on the account?
- Did the customer recently upgrade, restart, or cancel a subscription?
- Does the charge appear on the product billing page?
- Is the user asking for explanation, refund review, or cancellation help?

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Confirm the charge date, amount, and account email.
2. Review the customer's current plan and billing history, if access allows.
3. Check whether the charge matches renewal, upgrade, add-on, usage, or tax.
4. Explain the likely reason in plain language.
5. Escalate to billing if the charge does not match account records or requires refund approval.

## Customer-Facing Response

I can help review this. Please share the charge date, amount, and the email address on the account. I will compare that with the account billing history and explain what the charge appears to be. If it does not match the account records, I will escalate it for billing review.

If multiple customers misunderstand the charge, review billing-page copy, renewal reminders, cancellation confirmation, and invoice labels. Create a usability finding if the billing explanation is unclear.
