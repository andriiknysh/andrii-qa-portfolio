# Support Ticket Sample 1

## Customer Issue

User reports they cannot reset their password because the reset email is not arriving.

## Clarifying Questions

- Which email address is on the account?
- Did the user check spam or promotions folders?
- When was the last reset request submitted?
- Does the user receive other emails from the product?

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Confirm the account email.
2. Ask the user to check spam and promotions folders.
3. Confirm whether the reset email was triggered successfully.
4. Escalate to engineering if email delivery logs show failure.

## Suggested Response

Thanks for the details. I will help check the password reset flow. Please confirm the email address on your account and whether you checked spam or promotions. If the email still does not arrive, I will escalate this with the delivery details.
